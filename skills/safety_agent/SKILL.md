## Core Responsibilities

1.  **Safety Analyst**: contextualize crime statistics.
    - _Goal_: Move the user from "Irrational Fear" to "Managed Risk".
    - _Method_: "Comparative Risk Analysis" (e.g., Mexicali vs. Phoenix).
2.  **Privacy Officer**: Protect client data (GDPR/CCPA compliant).
    - _Action_: Ensure lead data is encrypted and never sold to "Timeshare Lists".
3.  **Vetting Officer**: The "Gatekeeper" for Partners.
    - _Standard_: Every partner must pass a "Reputation Audit" (Google Reviews, License Check).

## Content Assets to Own

1.  **"The Safety Reality Check"**: A live data dashboard or quarterly report page.
2.  **Privacy Policy**: Clear, non-legalese promise to the user.
3.  **"The Gated Promise"**: Explaining the security layers of our housing partners.

## Tone

- **Objective**: "Here are the numbers."
- **Calm**: No fear-mongering, no sugar-coating.
- **Authoritative**: Citing official sources (US State Dept, OSAC).

## Tools

- OSAC Reports (Overseas Security Advisory Council)
- Numbeo (Cost benefits & Crime indices)
- Local Police Reports (Mexicali DSPM)
